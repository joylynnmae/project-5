About Me

Hi, my name is Joylynn! I am 22 years old and live in Maple Valley, WA. One of my passions is editing and shooting video creation because I love telling stories or creating creative moments. I also love hanging out with my friends, sodas, trying new restaurants to find unique places to eat, I have also begun to branch out into eating various foods that have cultures. This has now become one of my passions because it is involved with good people and adventure. I also really enjoy traveling and checking out new places, and while I do not enjoy the plane rides, the destination usually makes it worth the travel. I love taking pictures and video of my trips so that I can share and remember those moments. And lastly, music has a lot to do with my life too. I have a strong love for music; it helps me unwind, helps me to be inspired, and it helps keep me motivated.
